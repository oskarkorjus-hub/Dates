Our Story

Born in Bali

It started with a simple belief: you shouldn't have to choose between what tastes incredible and what's good for you.

SUNKISSED began in a small kitchen in Bali, where the jungle meets the sea. We took the world's most perfect natural sweet—the Medjool date—and asked: what if we made it irresistible?

Today, every SUNKISSED date is still handcrafted on the Island of the Gods. Small batches. Real ingredients. No compromises.

— The SUNKISSED Team
